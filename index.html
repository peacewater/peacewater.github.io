How to use this site

This site contains many different water technology solutions to help with various water issues, such as storing drinking water and cleaning waste/sewer water. To find a solution tailored to your needs, just scroll down to the “Search” section. Type keywords related to your needs into the search bar (e.g., “rainwater”). You can also use the provided filters to narrow your results. Click “Submit search” to bring up relevant solutions. Click on any of the listed names to learn more about the listed solutions.

To learn more about the team that created this site or contact us, there is an “about us” tab above that will give more information. To browse all posted solutions, follow the “All solutions” tab at the top of the page. To learn more about the lifecycle of water, click “Water Diagrams” tab. If you can’t find what you need here, check out the “Resources” tab for other resources.
